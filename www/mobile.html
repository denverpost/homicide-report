About the Denver Homicide Report

In an attempt to put homicide numbers in better perspective, The Denver Post is collaborating with media outlets in similarly sized cities across the country: the El Paso Times in El Paso, Texas; the Courier-Journal in Louisville, Ky.; The Tennessean in Nashville, Tenn.; and The Oregonian in Portland, Ore. Journalists will track homicides in these cities and comparisons will be published once enough data is collected.

We will continue to add functionality to the Denver Homicide Report in the coming months. If you have questions or suggestions, please e-mail Joe Murphy or Kevin Hamm.

Anyone with information about these homicides should call the Denver Police Department at 720-913-6010.

This project was inspired by Homicide Watch DC.
