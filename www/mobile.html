About the Denver Homicide Report

We will continue to add functionality to the Denver Homicide Report in the coming months. If you have questions or suggestions, please e-mail Joe Murphy or Kevin Hamm.

Anyone with information about these homicides should call the Denver Police Department at 720-913-6010.

This project was inspired by Homicide Watch DC.

The map, table and chart track homicides in the City and County of Denver in 2015. Information is collected from news reports, the coroner at Denver's Office of the Medical Examiner and the Denver Police Department. Initial information may be incomplete and will be updated as it becomes available.

Homicides 2010-15

The chart shows cumulative monthly homicides — excluding vehicular homicides — from 2010 to 2015. It will be updated as information becomes available.
